## Overview

Janek Unon helps you create organized documentation spaces for your projects. Follow these steps to sign up, configure your first space, and publish your initial document. You complete the setup in under 5 minutes.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use your brand color `#3B82F6` in custom themes for a consistent look across your docs.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Start by signing up for a free account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://app.janek-unon.com`.

    Click **Sign Up** in the top right corner.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and verify your account via email.

    <Callout kind="info">
      Enable two-factor authentication immediately for security.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Select your workspace type (personal or team) and skip optional integrations for now.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Initial Space Setup

After logging in, create your first documentation space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    Click **New Space** from the dashboard.

    Name it `My Project Docs` and set visibility to public or private.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Settings" icon="settings">
    In space settings, add your brand color `#3B82F6` under **Theme**.

    Enable features like search and analytics.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Document

Publish a quickstart page to test your space.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Editor" icon="edit-3">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Add Page" icon="file-plus">
        Select **New Page** > **quickstart.mdx**.

        Use the built-in editor to add content.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Add Frontmatter" icon="code">
        Insert YAML frontmatter at the top:

````mdx
```yaml
---
title: My Quickstart
description: First document example.
---
```
````

      </Step>

      <Step title="Preview & Publish" icon="eye">
        Click **Preview** to check rendering, then **Publish**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="CLI Upload" icon="terminal">
    Install the CLI and upload from your terminal.

    <CodeGroup tabs="npm,yarn">
```bash
npm install -g @janek-unon/cli
```
```bash
yarn global add @janek-unon/cli
```
    </CodeGroup>

    Authenticate and upload:

````bash
janek login
janek space upload ./my-docs/ --space="My Project Docs"
````
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
